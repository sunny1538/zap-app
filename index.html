3. Orders & Payments

  • All prices are in Indian Rupees (₹) and inclusive of applicable taxes unless stated.
  • Orders once placed and confirmed cannot be cancelled except in cases of unavailability.
  • TildaBite accepts payments via UPI, Net Banking, and Debit/Credit Cards. All orders are prepaid.
  • In case of payment failure, the amount will be refunded to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • TildaBite is not responsible for delays caused by third-party payment gateways.

4. Delivery Policy

  • Estimated delivery times are indicative and may vary based on traffic, weather, and demand.
  • A 4-digit OTP will be shared with you. Please provide it to the delivery rider to confirm delivery.
  • TildaBite is not liable for incorrect delivery addresses provided by the customer.
  • Free delivery is currently offered on all orders subject to minimum order value.

5. Refunds & Cancellations

  • Refund requests must be raised within 30 minutes of delivery.
  • Once a request is raised, our team will contact you directly to understand the issue and negotiate a resolution (replacement, partial refund, or full refund).
  • Refund will only be processed after mutual agreement and confirmation from the customer. No refund will be issued without your explicit confirmation.
  • Approved refunds will be credited to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • Requests raised after 30 minutes of delivery will not be entertained.
